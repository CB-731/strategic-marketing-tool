## Checklists

### Task 1 — Marketing Fundamentals

- Identified my specific target market
- Defined exactly what they want
- Documented how the business helps them get it
- Written the statement: "We help [people] get [outcome] through [product]"
- Created the Business ↔ Marketing ↔ Target Market diagram

### Task 2 — How People Think

- Defined the Current State (frustrations, fears, daily feelings)
- Defined the Dream State (what they want to be/have/do)
- Listed all Roadblocks between current and dream
- Identified Solutions (methods that solve the roadblocks)
- Listed specific Products in each solution category
- Used the market's actual language throughout (not my words)

### Task 3 — Market Research

- Audited 3-5 direct local competitors (websites, ads, messaging)
- Found top players in major competitive markets (LA, NYC, SF)
- Documented what top players say AND what they don't say
- Completed the Top Player Funnel Breakdown template

### Task 4 — The CIA Method

- Found and confirmed an insider from the target market
- Prepared all interview questions before the call
- Recorded the call (with permission) and have transcript/notes
- Mapped their full journey (how they found out, why they bought)
- Captured exact quotes about fears, worries, and desires
- Battle-tested 2-3 ad concepts and documented their reactions
- Extracted insider language to use in copy

### Task 5 — The Winner's Writing Process

- Answered Q1: Who am I targeting? (specific, using Tasks 1–4 data)
- Answered Q2: Where are they now? (awareness level + attention type)
- Answered Q3: What one action do I want them to take?
- Answered Q4: What mental steps happen before they take that action?
- Answered Q5: What triggers each mental step?
- All 5 questions answered in order (not skipped)

### Task 6 — Where They Are Now

- Identified the awareness level (Unaware/Problem/Solution/Product Aware)
- Identified the attention type (Passive or Active)
- Mapped to the correct funnel type using the 2×2 grid
- Chosen the right platform based on the grid
- Written the moment statement
- Visualized the exact moment marketing hits them (time, place, mindset)

### Task 7 — The Three Thresholds

- Assessed Desire/Value threshold
- Assessed Certainty threshold
- Assessed Trust threshold
- Identified the weakest threshold
- Designed 3-5 specific triggers to raise Desire
- Designed 3-5 specific triggers to raise Certainty
- Designed 3-5 specific triggers to raise Trust
- Matched threshold requirements to the size of the ask

### Task 8 — Triggers, Effects & The Baton Handoff

- Listed every mental step from WWP Q4
- Designed a specific trigger for each mental step
- Mapped each trigger to its marketing element
- Verified the Ad hands off cleanly to the Landing Page
- Verified the Landing Page hands off cleanly to the Offer
- Read the entire funnel top to bottom out loud — flows as one thought
- Reverse engineered 2-3 top player funnels for reference

### Task 9 — Drafting

- Reviewed WWP outline before writing
- Pictured the specific target person from CIA interview
- Wrote 2-3 variations of each asset
- Kept the inner critic off during drafting
- Used voice-to-text or fast typing — no stopping
- Worked on one element at a time
- Wrote in a distraction-free environment

### Task 10 — Self-Review: Three Scans

- Waited before reviewing (took a break — walk, sleep, reset)
- Lizard Brain Scan complete — removed boring/confusing parts
- Baton Handoff Scan complete — every line leads to the next
- Threshold Scan complete — Desire, Certainty, Trust above the line
- Language precision — used market's exact words from CIA notes
- Cut ruthlessly — deleted everything not pushing toward the action
- Checked mechanics: typos, links, images, buttons all work

### Task 11 — CIA Insider Review

- Showed comparisons: my version + 2-3 competitor versions side by side
- Reviewed via video to watch body language and facial reactions
- Noted where insider hesitated, tensed up, or skimmed
- Asked about which threshold was weak when they hesitated
- Rewarded negative feedback to keep truth flowing
- Iterated based on feedback and re-showed
- Insider confirmed "I'd pick this one"

### Task 12 — The Iteration Cycle

- Ran all three self-review scans on the draft
- Edited and re-scanned until all three passed
- Sent to CIA insider with comparisons
- Edited based on insider feedback
- Re-showed to insider
- Insider confirmed preference for my version
- All assets are launch-ready and pre-validated
- Ready to launch
